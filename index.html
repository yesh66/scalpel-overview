01 · Why now

Surgery is being rebuilt in real time — the backend still runs on paper.

Volumes up 5–7% a year. Robotic platforms taking over the table. Instrument complexity has roughly tripled in a decade.

Meanwhile, the surgical supply chain — from warehouses and couriers to sterile services — the backend that readies every procedure, still runs on paper, human memory, and fragmented spreadsheets.

The gap between what the OR needs and what the backend can deliver is now the single biggest drag on hospital surgical capacity. And it widens every quarter.
